Why Empathy Matters in the AI Age

While AI can process information and respond to queries, it cannot truly understand human emotions, experiences, and perspectives. This module will help you develop and strengthen your capacity for genuine empathy and human connection—skills that will become increasingly valuable as AI becomes more sophisticated.
